About FixIT

FixIT exists for one reason: getting a real tech problem solved shouldn't require knowing tech.

Most platforms expect you to already speak the language — to know whether you need "hardware support" or a "backend engineer." We flipped that around. On FixIT you describe what's wrong in plain words, and a real person takes it from there.

How we're different

  • No jargon. Tap what sounds like your problem, type a sentence, optionally add a photo. That's it.
  • A human in the loop. A manager reviews every request, agrees on a fair price, and explains it in words anyone can understand.
  • The right specialist. Approved jobs go out to qualified fixers, and the first available one picks it up.
  • You stay in control. Nothing is final until you confirm the work is done.

From a blue screen to a brand-new website, FixIT connects everyday people with IT pros who actually want to help.

Trust & safety

We want FixIT to feel safe for people who aren't sure who to trust with their tech.

  • Every job is reviewed by a manager. A real person checks your request before any fixer sees it.
  • Clear, explained pricing. You name your budget; if the manager suggests a different price, they tell you exactly why. You can accept or decline.
  • Qualified fixers only. Jobs are matched to specialists, and a fixer must accept before starting.
  • You confirm completion. A job is only marked done after you say it's fixed.
  • Cancel anytime. Fixed it yourself? You can call off a request before it's completed.

We only collect what's needed to solve your problem — your contact details and what you tell us about the issue. Photos you upload are used solely to help a fixer understand the problem.
